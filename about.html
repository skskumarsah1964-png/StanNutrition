Our Story

We Started With A Simple Mission

Stan Nutrition was born in 2023 with one goal — to make high-quality, effective, and affordable nutrition accessible to everyone in India.

Founded by fitness enthusiasts and certified nutritionists, we noticed that most supplements available in the market were either overpriced imports or filled with cheap fillers. We decided to change that.

Today, thousands of customers across India trust Stan Nutrition for their daily protein, vitamins, pre-workouts, and wellness needs because we never compromise on quality.

We test every batch in FSSAI & ISO-certified labs and only use premium ingredients sourced globally.
